Delete your Travel Feed account

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Travel Feed (developer: Ram Saladi) lets you permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app, at any time, without needing to contact support.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Travel Feed app and log in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the two warning prompts that appear.

Your account will be permanently deleted immediately. This action cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes the following from our systems:

  • Your profile (username, bio, avatar)
  • All trips and stops you created
  • All photos you uploaded
  • All posts, comments, and likes you made
  • All friend connections and pending friend requests

This action is immediate and permanent. We do not retain a recovery window or backup copy of deleted account data after deletion completes.
