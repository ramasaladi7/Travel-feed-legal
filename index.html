Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Travel Feed ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application that lets you log stops during your trips and share them with friends. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Account information: your email address, username, and password (stored securely and never visible to us in plain text) when you create an account.
  • Profile information: any name, bio, or avatar photo you choose to add to your profile.
  • Location data: GPS coordinates you choose to attach to a stop when logging a trip. Location is only collected when you actively tap to add it — the app does not track your location in the background.
  • Photos: photos you choose to upload from your camera or photo library to attach to a trip stop.
  • Content you create: trip details, stop notes, ratings, captions, comments, and likes.
  • Friend connections: information about friend requests you send or receive within the app.

How we use your information

  • To operate core features: creating trips, logging stops, sharing posts with friends, and displaying your content to people you're connected with.
  • To enable account access, password resets, and email confirmations.
  • To improve the reliability and performance of the app.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties, and we do not use your data for targeted advertising.

Who can see your information

Trips and stops you create are private by default. When you choose to "post" a stop to the feed, it becomes visible to your accepted friends within the app. Your username and profile information are visible to other users so they can find and add you as a friend.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Supabase: for authentication, database storage, and file storage (photos).
  • Resend: for sending account-related emails (confirmation, password reset).
  • Pexels: for decorative background imagery shown in parts of the app interface. No personal data is sent to Pexels.

These providers may process data on our behalf under their own privacy and security practices.

Data retention and deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time from the Profile screen in the app. Deleting your account removes your profile, trips, stops, photos, posts, comments, likes, and friend connections from our systems. This action cannot be undone.

Children's privacy

Travel Feed is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
